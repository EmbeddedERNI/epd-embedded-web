About ERNI Embedded Community

ERNI Embedded community starts in January 2017 as an initiative to gather and expand the knowledge on embedded systems of ERNIans. The aim of the Embedded community is to establish a communication channel between experts on different technologies with people willing to learn about them. This is achieved through:

  • Regular meeting to discuss new technologies and implementations based on embedded systems

  • Forum to express doubts and share knowledge between ERNIans

  • Practical workshops to implement and test acquired knowledge with expert mentoring

  • Open doors to project proposals and technology opportunities to expand market and solutions portfolio of ERNI

If you feel in need or have a new idea to be explored, do not hesitate to contact the community
